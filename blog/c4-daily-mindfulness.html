The philosophy of 'doing less than you can' and 'taking one breath each day' resonate better with me than the 'have a gym buddy for mindfulness'. Though I do question the focus on meditation-based mindfulness - I really think the most important thing is to be really aware and really living your own life - whiskers on kittens and parcels tied up with string (but not kittens tied up with string) rather than three minutes of deep breathing. Well, maybe it's not an either/or so much as a both/and scenario?
